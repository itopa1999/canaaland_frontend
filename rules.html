LIFE DURING CONFERENCE

But the Lord is in His Holy temple, let all earth keep silence before Him -Hebrews 2:20
Thy testimonies are very sure; holiness becometh thine house,O LORD, forever -Psalms 93:5.

YOU ARE WELCOME TO CAC CANAANLAND INTERDENOMINATION YOUTH POWER EXPLOSION CONFERENCE 2024

May the peace, love and joy of our Lord Jesus Christ be greatly multiplied unto you as you participate in this year's Conference. Amen.
Let us allow the Holy Spirit to lead us in abiding by the following Conference rules:

  • All participants are expected to REGISTER as soon as they arrive in the Camp and ALWAYS put on the Conference badge.(Ezra 2:62,Matthew 22:11-13).
  • All participants on this camp ground are STRONGLY ENJOINED to attend all sessions of the conference as scheduled in the programme.
  • Nobody SHOULD stay behind in the room during programme for any reason such as SLEEPING,COOKING or PRAYING etc. Anyone not feeling fine should immediately report to his/her COORDINATOR.
  • Kindly avoid every act of MISCONDUCT Such as:
    1. Immodest dressing: such as wearing transparent and/or unnecessarily tight dresses.1 Timothy 2:9,22,Acts 15:20
    2. Stealing/pilfering
    3. Use of foul language and foolish talk. James 3:1-12, Proverbs18:21,1Timothy 4:12
    4. Disrespect to any Church Leader and Conference Officials.
    5. Quarrelling, bickering and fighting .
  • Halls of residence and rooms are out of bound to the opposite sex except with the express permission of the Conference Officials or Accommodation Officers.
  • Participants should not go out of the Camp ground, without the knowledge and permission of their COORDINATOR(S) or any acquaintance.
  • Be guided in your use of phones during programs. Be mature enough to switch it off when necessary.
  • Obey the instructions and direction of the Conference Officials, Ushers and Security personnel. They have our order to maintain orderliness within and outside the Conference halls.
  • Report all cases of accidents or incidents immediately to the information centre. Movement on the altar is highly restricted.
  • Maintain a very neat environment both in the hostels and in the lecture halls.
  • Keep your belongings well and safe, and in case of any lost-but-found items, report to the information Centre or Conference Officers.
  • Flee Youthful lust...and every appearance of evil. Abide in holiness.(Psalms 95:5).
  • Participants should endeavour to wait till the end of the Conference so as to receive Godâ€™s blessings in full.
  • Let us earnestly live as if rapture will take place in this Conference.
  • If you know these things you shall be blessed indeed if you do them. (John 13:17). If you are willing and obedient, YOU shall eat the good of the land. Isaiah 1:19.

    • GOD BLESS YOU!
